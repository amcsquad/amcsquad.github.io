About the Petts Wood May Queen Group

The Petts Wood May Queen Group is a community group that, each year, comes together to crown our May Queen and participate in May Fayres throughout South London and Kent.

Rehearsals are on Thursday 4.30 pm at thePetts Wood Methodist Church,Queensway, Petts Wood, Orpington BR5 1EB
